Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 17, 2025

BrightBins Sprint (the “App”) is a mobile application developed by Battung Daniela (“we”, “us”, “our”). We respect your privacy and process data transparently and minimally—only as needed to operate the App and comply with Apple’s platform requirements.

1. Scope

This Policy explains what data the App may process on Apple devices, how that data is used, shared, and protected. By using the App, you agree to this Policy.

2. Data We Process

  • Data you provide: support requests, feedback, attachments, and any information you knowingly send to us via email or contact forms.
  • In‑App Purchases (if applicable): purchase facts and subscription status. We do not receive your payment card details; payments are processed by Apple via the App Store.
  • Apple diagnostics and analytics: aggregated crash/diagnostic and performance reports Apple may provide to developers (e.g., App Store Connect Analytics). These reports do not directly identify you and are used only to improve stability and quality.

We do not collect advertising identifiers (IDFA), precise geolocation, or the contents of your personal files/media outside the App’s core functionality. We do not share data with third‑party ad networks.

3. Purposes of Processing

  • Provide and improve the App’s functionality;
  • Offer user support and respond to requests;
  • Process purchases and restore access to previously purchased features;
  • Comply with legal obligations and Apple platform requirements.

4. Legal Bases

We process data based on: (i) performance of a contract with the user (providing the App), (ii) our legitimate interest in improving stability and quality, and (iii) legal obligations (e.g., accounting/tax records of purchases).

5. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary for the purposes above: correspondence until a request is resolved and for a reasonable archival period; purchase records for legally required periods. Anonymous diagnostic reports may be retained by Apple under their policies.

6. Sharing with Third Parties

  • Apple — billing and distribution via the App Store, analytics and diagnostics reports.
  • Service providers (e.g., email/support tools), if and when used. We require them to maintain appropriate confidentiality and security.

We do not sell personal data and do not share it for targeted advertising.

7. Security

We use reasonable organizational and technical safeguards. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

8. Children

The App is not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child provided us data without parental consent, contact us and we will delete such data.

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict processing, or obtain a copy of your data. To exercise these rights, contact us using the email below.
